"Eye-Lash" polymorph(s)


Exemplary 2D class averages of eye-lash polymorph(s)

  • Asymmetric polymorphs with very thin and very wide regions visible in the micrographs and 2D class averages (similar to eye lashes, which indicates an oblate cross-section
  • The appearance of this polymorph has too our knowledge not been described in the literature. We compared the present 2D classes to several others 2D classes of whichever amyloid fibril in the literature and couldn't find a correspondence
  • Right now we are working on obtaining a 3D reconstruction
  • Might be more than one polymorph
